# Timeline & Board

The Timeline (center area) is the main workspace where cards representing Epics, Features and other work items are placed on a time axis.

Card anatomy
- Header: title, type, and quick metadata.
- Body: short description and allocation markers.
- Resize handle: drag to change duration.

Common interactions
- Drag horizontally to move start/target dates.
- Drag the right handle to resize duration.
- Click a card to open the Details panel.
- Drag the background to pan the view; use the zoom shortcuts to change scale.

Dependencies
- Enable Dependencies in View Options to draw links between related cards.

Zoom & Scale
- Use buttons on the timeline Scale control in the Sidebar to switch zoom levels.

Best practices
- Use Compact Cards for overviews and expand when you need details.
- Combine timeline scale with filters to focus on a specific time window and team.
